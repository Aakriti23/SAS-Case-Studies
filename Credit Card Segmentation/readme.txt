Credit Card Spending

Business Objective: 
The dataset summarizes the usage behavior of about 9000 active credit card holders. We are to develop a customer segmentation to define marketing strategy and use derived KPIs to gain insights on the customer profiles. Furthermore, apply a data reduction techinique for variable reduction and a clustering algorithm to reveal the behavioural segments of credit card holders and provide strategic insights and implementation of strategies for given set of cluster characterstics.
